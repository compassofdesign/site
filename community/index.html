The Community Chat

The community is open 24/7. We have people from all around the world sharing work they've done and giving feedback to others. These are like-minded individuals who are focused on becoming better at design. So should you have any questions, we are capable of handling them.

We have categories like:

  • #Design-Review: As a member of the community, you can seek good feedback on your work while it's in progress. We'll help where we can to take your design to the next level.
  • #Resources: Keep updated with all sorts of great resources that you and others find outside of the community for designers.
  • #Work: Here, you can collaborate with other members, seek new job opportunities, or hire freelancers to help you.
  • #Today I Made: Share something you've made today, if you were a part of it, be proud! We can join in the fun with likes, comments, etc.

Design Feedback

As a designer, one of the hardest things to get is feedback that is actually helpful for your design work and portfolio.
