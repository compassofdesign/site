So what is it?

This is an online community with some of the best designers around.

Whether you want to bounce ideas off other designers, learn the best practices, get tips and tricks, or just relax with other people who understand the struggle, we want to help.

The Community Chat

The community chat is open 24/7. We have people from all around the world in the group. These are like-minded individuals who are focused on becoming better at design. So should you have any questions, we are capable of handling them.

Some of the topics include:

  • #Design-Review: This channel is for seeking design feedback, posting your work and bouncing around ideas that you are looking to have reviewed
  • #Resources: This channel is for posting design resources and links to articles that you find helpful and would like to share.
  • #Work: This channel is for posting any collaboration requests, hiring freelancers, posting design job openings, etc.
  • #Introductions: Say hello to the people around you. Great topics are: what you do, where you're from, how you got into design, favorite pizza toppings?
  • #Today I Made: Share something you've made today, if you were a part of it, be proud! We can join in the fun with likes, comments, etc.

Design Feedback

As a designer, one of the hardest things to get is helpful feedback on your design work and portfolio. We have built the community around giving constructive feedback.

When you join, you immediately have access to that. What you'll find is that not only does seeking feedback help you get better at design, but giving good feedback helps you grow as a designer as well.

The Community Publication

Every week, we take some of the content from the newsletter and put it out to the public. These are old topics from older newsletters, so you will always receive the freshest content on the newsletter.

The publication is here for community members too. Should you do any writing around design, productivity, creative output, etc... you can request that your article be featured on the publication!
