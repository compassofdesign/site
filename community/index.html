Who we are

We are the designers like you.

We are: Self-Taught, Self-Starters, Parents With Side Businesses, Design Students, Web Designers and Developers, Agency Owners, People in the Hustle and Grind, the Designer.

It can be difficult starting out without an idea of where to go for design help. When you work on your own, there's not a lot of people around you who you can bounce ideas off of.

We're building a community to fix that.

Whether you want to bounce ideas off other designers, learn the best practices, get tips and tricks, or just relax with other people who understand the struggle, we want to help.

Join the community of self start designers who want to level up in their career.

Popular Topics

#Design-Review

This channel is for seeking design feedback, posting your work and bouncing around ideas that you are looking to have reviewed

#Resources

This channel is for posting design resources and links to articles that you find helpful and would like to share.

#Work

This channel is for posting any collaboration requests, hiring freelancers, posting design job openings, etc.

#Introductions

Say hello to the people around you. Great topics are: what you do, where you're from, how you got into design, favorite pizza toppings? (I identify as one who loves pineapple on pizza)
