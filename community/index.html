The Community Chat

The community chat is open 24/7. We have people from all around the world connecting and making new friends. These are like-minded individuals who are focused on becoming better at design. So should you have any questions, we are capable of handling them.

Some of the topics include:

  • #Design-Review: As a member of the community, you have other designers aroun you who are able to give good feedback on your work while it's in progress.
  • #Resources: Keep updated with all sorts of great resources that you and others find outside of the community for designers.
  • #Work: Here, you can collaborate with other members, seek new job opportunities, or hire freelancers to help you.
  • #Today I Made: Share something you've made today, if you were a part of it, be proud! We can join in the fun with likes, comments, etc.

Design Feedback

As a designer, one of the hardest things to get is helpful feedback on your design work and portfolio. We have built the community around giving constructive feedback.

When you join, you immediately have access to that. What you'll find is that not only does seeking feedback help you get better at design, but giving good feedback helps you grow as a designer as well.
