The grading scale

Your skills as a designer will continue to grow as you continue to feed them with education and practice. It is not expected for you to show up knowing everything, and even then, designers who know everything can be really difficult to collaborate with. Knowing your competence level is the perfect way for you to be able to work objectively.

The scale is built off of the Conscious Competence ladder. Based on the ladder, there are 4 levels of expertise as you are learning a new skill.

  • Beginner: You don't know the skill exists and are happy going about your life.
  • Competent: You are now aware this skill, but you are unable to use it.
  • Proficient: This is a skill you can now use, but with a lot of effort.
  • Skilled: You are able to use this skill without even having to try.
